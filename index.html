The Eternal Prayer

Navkar Mahamantra

णमो अरिहंताणं
Namo Arihantāṇaṁ
णमो सिद्धाणं
Namo Siddhāṇaṁ
णमो आयरियाणं
Namo Āyariyāṇaṁ
णमो उवज्झायाणं
Namo Uvajjhāyāṇaṁ
णमो लोए सव्वसाहूणं
Namo Loe Savvasāhūṇaṁ

एसो पंच णमोक्कारो, सव्वपावप्पणासणो।
मंगलाणं च सव्वेसिं, पढमं हवइ मंगलं॥

I bow to the Arihantas, the Siddhas, the Acharyas, the Upadhyayas and all the Sadhus of the world. This fivefold salutation destroys all sin and is the foremost of all that is auspicious.

The Five Vows of Bhagwan Mahavir

Timeless principles, for every seeker

Mahavirpuram carries the invaluable sermons of Bhagwan Mahavir to the entire world — not for one community alone, but for students, thinkers and all humankind.

Ahimsa

Non-violence

Compassion toward every living being in thought, word and deed.

Satya

Truth

A life rooted in honesty and inner clarity.

Asteya

Non-stealing

Integrity that takes nothing which is not freely given.

Brahmacharya

Self-discipline

Mastery of the senses and purity of conduct.

Aparigraha

Non-attachment

Freedom from possessiveness and the weight of desire.
